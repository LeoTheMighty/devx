## Goal

Ship a `/devx-init` that puts any repo (empty or existing) onto the devx rails ‚Äî backlog files, config, OS supervisor units, CLI skeleton ‚Äî without yet running an execution loop. After Phase 0, the *shape* of the system is visible end-to-end even though no agents have spawned.
